Origin Story

We opened our main branch in Morrisville, NC, in October of 2018, just before the Covid 19 epidemic. It was a scary time, but we had faith and knew our food was delicious and people would enjoy it. We were right! The epidemic actually made our restaurant stronger due to a strong take out orders. Now we are four branches and still going strong.
